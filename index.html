Agenda Themes

Eight themes. One strategic conversation.

01

National cyber governance & strategy

How nations organize resilience at scale, aligning ministries, regulators, authorities, and private operators.

02

Critical infrastructure protection

Energy, water, telecom, finance, transport, healthcare and smart-city systems; OT/ICS security and continuity for essential services.

03

AI & emerging threats

AI-enabled attacks, deepfakes and identity fraud, securing AI-powered organizations, AI-security governance.

04

Incident response, continuity & recovery

National-level coordination, crisis communications, public-private response models, recovery and business-continuity planning.

05

Cyber diplomacy & cross-border coordination

Regional alliances, CERT-to-CERT cooperation, supply-chain and third-party risk.

06

Cloud, identity & data resilience

Sovereign cloud, data residency, zero-trust architecture, protecting national digital-identity systems.

07

Boardroom cyber resilience

Moving cyber risk from technical teams to boards, reporting, insurance, risk quantification, executive accountability.

08

The future of trusted innovation

What governments and operators need from vendors, and how to sell responsibly into regulated sectors.

FAQ

Common questions.

Who should attend?

National Resilience is built for government and national cyber leaders, regulators, critical-infrastructure operators, and enterprise security leadership, CISOs, CIOs, and the executives responsible for resilience, risk, and continuity, alongside selected technology and advisory partners.

Is this an official GISEC event?

No. National Resilience is an independent executive forum produced by More & More, held in Dubai during GISEC Global 2026 week. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by GISEC.

Where and when is it?

Dubai, on 17 September 2026, 17:00–21:00, with an optional VIP dinner afterward. The venue will be announced closer to the date.

How curated is the audience?

Highly. Attendance is invitation-only and approved by RSVP, with a curated room of roughly 300–350 senior decision-makers, quality of conversation over volume.

Can my company partner or sponsor?

Yes. Strategic, Executive Showcase, Knowledge, and Supporting partnerships are available, along with VIP Dinner and Meeting Lounge add-ons. Use Become a Partner and our team will follow up.

Are there private meeting rooms?

Yes. Sponsor meeting rooms are available on-site for partners to host high-value conversations away from the main floor.

Is there an executive roundtable?

Yes. A small, private, high-trust roundtable for 20–35 senior leaders runs alongside the forum, with an optional closed VIP dinner.

Can I apply to speak?

Yes. We curate operators, government voices, and senior technology leaders. Use Nominate a Speaker and our team will review every submission.

Is there a cost to attend?

No. Approved attendance is complimentary, on an invitation-only basis.

When does registration open?

Attendance is curated. Request an invitation now and our team will review and confirm your place with next steps.
